Link of Hyrule

Link

Champion and Hero of Hyrule

  • His look is inspired by Peter Pan.
  • Link is left-handed.
  • He is depicted as both a child and adult.
  • Young Link is voiced by a female.
  • He is quite the musician.
  • He is linked to the triforce of courage.
  • Link has a female couterpart, Linkle.
  • His name, Link, is in reference to the link between you and the adventure.
  • Portrayed as a humble but brave hero, his qualities are inconsistent with being a proper hero, or the bearere of the Triforce of Courage.
  • The Indiana Jones movies were a big inspiration for the original Zelda games and Link's sense of adventure.
